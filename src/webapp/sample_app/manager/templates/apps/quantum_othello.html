Basic Rule!

  1. 2 Players indicated with state |0> and |1>
  2. N x N grid, 6 possible gate operations.
  3. Objective: Optimize your own eigenbasis (|0> or |1>) using the given gate operations.
  4. The game contains 2 phases,
  5. Initialization: Players appoint the initial state to a qubit on the grid turn by turn.
  6. Operation: Each turns players are operating 1 of given gates to any qubit to maximize measurement outcome of their own state.
  7. Players are not allowed to operate on the qubit that was operated in previous turn.

Let's Play! Please choose the size of the board!
