Quantum Othello

Starting with NxN board, as usual, two players (or teams) can put their disc on the board in either superposition form or one of the state, white or black. Moreover, instead of just put in the dice, you can do quantum operation too! e.g. Hadamard, X, CX, or funny custom quantum gate to deal with the amplitude of the state! After 20 turns or so, the circuit created by players will then send to execute to get the result of the game!

Choose how many qubits do you want to use?
